# Welcome to OpenFeed

OpenFeed helps you collect user feedback, manage your product roadmap, and publish changelog updates — all through a single widget that lives inside your app.

You install it once with one line of code. After that, everything is managed from your dashboard. Change colors, enable or disable tabs, write changelog entries, respond to feedback — none of it requires touching your codebase again.

## What's included

**Feedback board**
Users submit feature requests and upvote existing ones without leaving your app. Before a submission is saved, AI checks for semantic duplicates using pgvector — so your board stays organized automatically. Anonymous users are supported through secure cookies, no login required.

**Public roadmap**
A drag-and-drop kanban board with three columns: Planned, In Progress, and Done. Your users can see what you're working on and what's coming next. Your team manages it entirely from the dashboard.

**Changelog**
Publish product updates using a rich text editor. Categorize entries as new feature, improvement, or bug fix. When a new entry is published, a notification dot appears on the widget button to let users know something changed.

**Announcements**
Show a banner inside your app — above or below the page — without modifying your codebase. Control the text, colors, link, and dismiss behavior entirely from your dashboard.

**AI Product Advisor**
An agentic AI that reads your actual feedback data before answering. Ask "what should I build next?" and it will check your most requested features, review your current roadmap, and give you a specific recommendation — not a generic answer.

**Analytics**
Track widget opens over time, see upvote trends, and identify your most requested features — all from a single dashboard view.

## How it works in one sentence

You add a script tag to your app, configure the widget from your dashboard, and OpenFeed handles everything else — collecting feedback, displaying your roadmap, notifying users about updates.

## Choose your setup

OpenFeed runs in two modes:

- **Cloud** — hosted by us at [openfeed.ink](https://app.openfeed.ink). Start with a 7-day free trial, no credit card required. [Get started →](/cloud)
- **Self-hosted** — run it on your own infrastructure using Docker. Free forever. [Self-hosting guide →](/self-hosting)
